SATIMGE.gms will automatically put 3 sets of results per case run (as defined in satimge.xlsm)
here in gdx format:
casename.gdx: TIMES annual results and some aggregate CGE results (if linked model was run)
H_casename.gdx: TIMES sub-annual results
REP2_casename.gdx: detailed CGE results (if linked model was run)
